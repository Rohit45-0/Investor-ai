# Impact Model

## Goal

Estimate the potential investor and workflow impact of Investor-ai using
explicit, back-of-the-envelope assumptions.

## What The Product Changes

Investor-ai reduces the time between a market event and an actionable investor
decision by combining:

- filing-led signal discovery
- technical confirmation
- grounded explanation
- auto-generated communication

## Assumptions

### User Assumptions

- Target cohort for early adoption: `1,000` active serious retail investors.
- Average manual market-scanning time today: `2 hours/day`.
- Average time after using Investor-ai: `20 minutes/day`.
- Trading / research days per year: `250`.

### Workflow Assumptions

- A typical user manually checks multiple disconnected sources:
  NSE disclosures, news summaries, price charts, and community chatter.
- Investor-ai centralizes this into one pipeline and four focused product surfaces.

### Opportunity Capture Assumptions

- Only `5%` of users act on a surfaced insight in a meaningful way each month.
- Of those actions, only `1` decision per month benefits materially from faster detection or better validation.
- Average value of that improved decision: `INR 2,000` per month per impacted user.

This is intentionally conservative and does not assume all alerts are tradable.

## Time Saved

Per user:

- Manual workflow: `120 min/day`
- Investor-ai workflow: `20 min/day`
- Daily savings: `100 min/day`

Annual savings per user:

- `100 min * 250 days = 25,000 minutes`
- `25,000 / 60 = 416.7 hours/year`

For `1,000` active users:

- `416,700 hours/year` saved

If we value investor time at a modest `INR 300/hour`:

- `416,700 * 300 = INR 125,010,000/year`

Estimated workflow-value impact:

- `~ INR 12.5 crore/year`

## Opportunity Value

Impacted users per month:

- `1,000 * 5% = 50 users`

Monthly financial improvement:

- `50 * INR 2,000 = INR 100,000/month`

Annualized:

- `INR 1,200,000/year`

Estimated direct decision-value impact:

- `~ INR 12 lakh/year`

## Media and Research Efficiency

For analysts, creators, or community operators using the Video Engine:

- Manual creation of a daily market briefing can take `45 to 90 minutes`.
- Investor-ai reduces that to payload refresh plus automated render oversight.
- Assuming `1` briefing per market day and `60 minutes` saved:
  - `250 hours/year` saved per operator

This matters because the same intelligence layer can be published as alerts,
chat answers, and video without separate manual editorial work.

## Why The Model Is Credible

- It uses conservative user counts for an early-stage product.
- It separates time savings from direct financial upside.
- It does not assume every alert converts into a trade.
- It recognizes that the biggest value may come from prioritization and focus,
  not prediction alone.

## Summary

Under modest assumptions, Investor-ai can plausibly create:

- `~416,700 hours/year` of workflow savings across `1,000` active users
- `~INR 12.5 crore/year` in time-value efficiency
- `~INR 12 lakh/year` in direct improved-decision value

The strategic value is larger than these numbers suggest because the platform
turns fragmented market data into one reusable intelligence layer that powers
signals, charts, chat, and video from the same source of truth.
